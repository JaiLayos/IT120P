ABOUT THE SYSTEM

This system will provide residents with an Air Quality Monitoring System that can monitor air reliability and assess the present air quality. A network of detectors is planned to collect data, and the resulting data is expected to be displayed on a portable or web-based server. This technology has the potential to identify contaminated issue areas and make sense of the spatial changes taking place there. If the data is made available, universities and research centers can use existing statistics to develop strategies for preventing and restoring air quality in the area.

OUR OBJECTIVES

This investigation aims to document the intensity of environmental contaminants to measure air quality levels and develop planning processes in the event of elevated concentrations. It will also identify problematic locations of pollution and get an understanding of the spatial shifts associated with them. Finally, it will develop an action plan to inform local residents about the state of the air quality in their area.
